Faculty-in-Charge

Dr. Rabindra Kumar Jena

"A professor in the area of Business Analytics & IT (BAIT) at the Institute of Management Technology, Nagpur, India. He has degrees in M.Sc., M.Tech(CSE), and Ph.D.(IT) from the Indian Institute of Information Technology and Management, Gwalior, India. He has more than 25 years of experience in teaching and research. He has over 100 research publications in various refereed international journals and conferences. He is the author of four books in the area of Information Technology. He is a guest editor for various reputed journals. He is on the editorial board of many reputed national and international journals. His areas of interest include technology adoption, soft computing techniques, and business analytics
He has been acknowledged in the Top 2% of scientists globally for both individual years and his career by Elsevier and Stanford University for the past four consecutive years (2022, 2023, 2024, 2025), and additionally ranked among the Top 5% of scientists worldwide by the SciRank Global registry, based on meticulous bibliometric analysis."
